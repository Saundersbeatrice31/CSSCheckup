First Job As A Developer

Best practices for new developers after bootcamp.


To Do List

  • Unpuff your resume
  • practice until you can live code on the spot.
  • study up
  • Build a porfolio
  • Work on your people skills.

Skills to master

Doing real deliberate practice with laser focus and a game plan. You need to put in about 1000 hours of coding exercises and another 300 in building applications.

People skills: You need to learn how to collaborate, communicate and present yourself well.

  • In addition to the above two skills. Real practice time will make a difference.
  • Building a solid portfolio is very important.
  • Do not be afraid to ask for help. When you need it.
